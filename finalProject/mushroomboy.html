Mushroom Boy

HEAD

Round 1: 6sc in mr [6]
Round 2: *inc* x6 [12]
Round 3: *sc, inc* x6 [18]
Round 4: *inc, 2sc* x6 [24]
Round 5: *3sc, inc* x6 [30]
Round 6: *sc, inc, (4sc, inc) x5, 3sc* [36]
Round 7: *5sc, inc* x6 [42]
Round 8: *2sc, inc, (6sc, inc) x5, 4sc* [48]
Round 9: *7sc, inc* x6 [54]
Round 10: *3sc, inc, (8sc, inc) x5, 5sc* [60]
Round 11: *9sc, inc* x6 [60]
Round 12-22: *66sc* [66]
Round 23: *9sc, dec* x6 [60]
Round 24: *60sc* [60]
Round 25: *8sc, dec* x6 [54]
Round 26: *3sc, dec, (7sc, dec) x5, 4sc* [48]
Round 27: *6sc, dec* x6 [42]
Round 28: *2sc, dec, (5sc, dec) x5, 3sc* [36]
Round 29: *4sc, dec* x6 [30]
Round 30: *3sc, dec* x6 [24]
Round 31: *2sc, dec* x6 [18]
Fasten off the yarn, stuff the head, and sew the eyes at rows 17-18, leaving 6 stitch space between them.

PATCHES (MAKE 7)

Use white yarn
Round 1: 6sc in a mr [6]
Round 2: *inc* x6 [12]
Fasten off the yarn, leaving a long tail for sewing.
*Stuff the hat about halfway before sewing on the patches

LEGS (MAKE 2)

Start the cream yarn
Round 1: 6sc in mr [6]
Round 2: *sc, inc* x3 [9]
Round 3: *inc, 2sc* x3 [12]
Round 4: *3sc, inc* x3 [15]
Round 5: *sc, inc, (4sc, inc) x2, 3sc* [18]
Make the second leg, change to red yarn and join first leg to make the body.

BODY

Round 6: *18sc* of thhe first leg + *18sc* of the second leg [36]
Round 7: *5sc, inc* x6 [42]
Round 8-12: *42sc* [42]
Change to cream yarn
Round 13: *42sc BLO* [42]
*BLO = Back Loop Only aka only sc in the inside part of the V
Round 14-15: *42sc* [42]
Round 16: *5sc, dec* x6 [36]
Round 17: *36sc* [36]
Round 18: *4sc, dec* x6 [30]
Round 19: *30sc* [30]
Round 20: *3sc, dec* x6 [24]
Round 21: *24sc* [24]
Fasten off the yarn, leaving a long tail for sewing. Stuff the body.

ARMS

Use cream yarn
Round 1: 6sc in mr [6]
Round 2: *sc, inc* x3 [9]
Round 3-11: *9sc* [9]
Do not stuff the arm. Fasten off the yarn, leaving a long tail for sewing. Fold the top of the arm but sew only the inner edges and then the outer edge to sew the body.

OVERALLS

Use pins to mark 9sc in the middle of the front of the pants.
Round 1: *9sc, ch 1* turn [9]
Round 2-5: *9sc, ch 1* turn [9]
Round 6: *dec, 5sc, dec* [7]
Do not cut yarn.
Make suspenders : ch 16 and connect the back of the trousers.
