Pear Bear

HEAD & BODY

Round 1: 6sc into mr
Round 2: *inc* x6 [12]
*Repeat stitch 6 times
Round 3: *sc, inc* x6 [18]
Round 4: *2sc, inc* x6 [24]
Round 5-8: 24sc [24]
Attach safety eyes between rounds 6 and 7, 4 or 5 st apart
Round 9: *3sc, inc* x6 [30]
Round 10: *4sc, inc* x6 [36]
Round 11: *5sc, inc* x6 [42]
Round 12: 42sc [42]
Round 13: *6sc, inc* x6 [48]
Round 14-19: *48sc* [48]
Round 20: *6sc, dec* [42]
Round 21: *5sc, dec* [36]
Round 22: *4sc, dec* x6 [30]
Round 23: *3sc, dec* x6 [24]
Round 24: *2sc, dec* x6 [18]
Stuff the head and body
Round 25: *sc, dec* x6 [12]
Round 26: *dec* x6 [6]
Cut long string of yarn and run end around the outside loops of last 6 stitches. Pull closed and weave in end.

ARMS

Round 1: 4sc in mr [4]
Round 2: *inc* x4 [8]
Round 3: 8sc [8]
Round 4: *2sc, dec* x2 [6]
Round 5-6: 6sc [6]
Single crochet the top of the arm closed. Slip stitch
and cut long string of yarn for sewing.

MUZZLE

Chain 5 [5]
Round 1: start in 2nd ch from the hook: 3sc, 3sc in same st, underside(2sc, inc, sl st to 1st sc)
Round 2: 10sc [10]
Close with sl st

LEAF

Chain 5 [5]
Round 1: sl st in 2nd ch from hook, sc, hdc, 6dc in last ch, underside(hdc, sc, sl st)

LEGS

Round 1: 5sc in mr [5]
Round 2: *inc* x5 [10]
Round 3: *sc, inc* x5 [15]
Round 4: 15sc [15]
Slip stitch and cut yarn for sewing.

EARS

Round 1: 8sc in mr (sl st into first sc) [10]
Cut yarn for sewing

STEM

Insert crochet hook into top of the head, and pull yarn loop through
Chain 7
Round 1: start in 2nd ch from hook: 6sc
Cut yarn and pull tail through, tie tails together and tuck in
